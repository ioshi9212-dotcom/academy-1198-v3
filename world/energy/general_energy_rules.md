# General Energy Rules

Energy is logical, physical, dangerous and visible through consequence.

Visible effects may include:

- heat;
- cold;
- pressure;
- light;
- vibration;
- sound;
- smell;
- body reaction;
- sensor reaction;
- environmental change.

Energy is not decorative magic. Describe what changes in the body, object, air, surface, sensor, smell, trajectory or social reaction.

## Academy background

In Academy scenes, energy exists as a constant social/physical background because students are carriers.

Not every background detail is a plot event, but every Academy scene should feel like it belongs to a place where energy control matters.

Examples:

- someone warms a drink with fingers;
- someone leaks cold when irritated;
- a bracelet blinks from a small spike;
- resonance makes a floor panel hum;
- a metal clip jerks under magnetic pressure;
- staff corrects an unauthorized display;
- a student hides shaking hands after control slips.

## Important

Small energy details must support the scene, not steal it.
Full demonstrations need trigger: training, conflict, check, accident, user action or calendar beat.
