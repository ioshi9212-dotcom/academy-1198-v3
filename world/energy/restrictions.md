# Energy Restrictions

Energy use must have limits, risk, visible cost, environmental constraints or control requirements.

## Academy restrictions

- Energy use outside allowed zones is a violation.
- Small slips are recorded as risk, not always punished immediately.
- Unauthorized showing off affects reputation/rating.
- Staff may stop a display with a surname, gesture, countermeasure or access mark.
- Sensors notice spikes, but they are not omniscient.
- Consequences can be social, procedural or physical.

## Limits

Every energy type has constraints:

- heat/fire: overheating, burns, smell, damaged surfaces, emotional spikes;
- cold/air: leakage reveals stress, heat weakens fine control;
- resonance: needs medium, noise can interfere, body cost through ears/voice/balance;
- magnetic: works mainly with metal, precision strain;
- impulse: wrong angle causes injuries;
- light: glare, overheating, sensory overload;
- suppression/gravity: fatigue, area limits, collateral pressure;
- spatial: rare, hard to classify, consequences may show before sensors understand.

## Scene rule

Energy cannot solve every access, social or combat problem without cost.
