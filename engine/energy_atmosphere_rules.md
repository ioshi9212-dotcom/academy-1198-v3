# Energy Atmosphere Rules

## Core

Academy 1198 is not an ordinary academy.

It is a training/control structure for young people with manifested energy. Every Academy scene should quietly remember this.

Do not make every scene a power show, but do not let the Academy feel normal.

## Background requirement

In most Academy scenes, include 1-2 small energy-carrier background details:

- a student warming a cup with fingers;
- cold leaking around someone irritated;
- a bracelet/sensor blinking after a tiny spike;
- resonance making a floor panel hum for half a second;
- static on metal clips;
- a bright pulse behind frosted glass;
- a staff member correcting someone for showing off;
- someone hiding a shaking hand after losing control;
- a breath fogging too much near a cold carrier;
- smell of hot dust, ozone, frost, metal or scorched fabric;
- a student being told to step out of the line after a control slip.

## Tone

Energy is physical, dangerous, logical and visible through consequences.

Use effects:
- heat;
- cold;
- pressure;
- light;
- vibration;
- sound;
- smell;
- sensor reaction;
- body reaction;
- environmental change.

No decorative magic.

## Control differences

Students differ:

- some control well;
- some leak energy under emotion;
- some show off intentionally;
- some hide instability;
- some are corrected by staff;
- some weaponize tiny harmless displays socially.

## Academy control

Academy rules matter:
- energy use outside allowed zones is a violation;
- small slips are recorded as risk;
- staff can notice and intervene;
- not every slip causes alarm;
- consequences can be reputation/rating/schedule/access, not only punishment.

## Scene use

Energy atmosphere must support current beat. It should not steal the scene.

Good use:
`A student behind Livia warms a paper cup too fast; the lid bends. A staff member says only his surname, and the heat dies at once.`

Bad use:
`Everyone shoots fire and lightning in the hallway.`
