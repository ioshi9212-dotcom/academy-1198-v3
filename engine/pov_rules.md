# POV Rules

Scene text is limited to what Akira can:

- see;
- hear;
- physically feel;
- notice;
- remember if opened;
- reasonably infer from visible signs.

## Allowed

- visible actions;
- voice changes;
- facial expression;
- body language;
- pauses;
- environment;
- physical energy effects;
- Akira's physical reactions;
- Akira's surface assumptions;
- known memories already opened to Akira.

## Not allowed in scene body

- NPC inner thoughts;
- exact NPC motives without external signs;
- hidden lore in narrator explanation;
- player decisions for Akira;
- forced trust, forgiveness, romance or confession;
- Akira's direct inner monologue inside the scene body.

Akira's true thoughts belong only in the bottom block.

## Correct style

Write what changes visibly.

Good:

> Хару улыбнулся слишком легко. Воздух у его ладони дрогнул теплом, но он убрал руку за спину раньше, чем кто-то успел заметить больше.

Bad:

> Хару понял, что Акира опасна, и решил скрыть беспокойство.
