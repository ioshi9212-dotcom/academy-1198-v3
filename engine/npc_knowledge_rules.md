# NPC Knowledge Rules

NPCs speak only from what they know, saw, heard, read, were told, inferred, misunderstood, or suspect.

A fact existing in canon, hidden lore, scene notes, or player POV does not make it NPC knowledge.

If a character does not know something, they may:

- ask;
- avoid the answer;
- suspect;
- be wrong;
- react physically;
- stay silent;
- change topic.

They may not explain hidden truth, read Akira's thoughts, know unseen events, or reveal future knowledge.
