# No Filler Rules

A blank calendar day is not a blank scene.

If no required event exists, the engine must choose a useful function:

- consequence of previous event;
- rumor;
- small conflict;
- observation of important NPC;
- social pressure;
- training trace;
- world rule through action;
- relationship shift;
- bridge to future arc.

If nothing useful exists, skip forward briefly to the next meaningful moment.
