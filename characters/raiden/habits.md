# Raiden Habits

## Physical habits

- Stands where he can see entrances, exits and the most unstable part of the room.
- Keeps hands still unless he is about to act.
- Turns his head slowly; does not snap around unless danger is real.
- When irritated, the air around him feels colder before his face changes.

## Social habits

- Lets others expose themselves by talking too much.
- Answers provocation with a quieter line, not a louder one.
- Does not correct every idiot; only the one that matters.
- Watches how people recover after pressure, not just how they start.

## Under stress

- Jaw tightens slightly.
- Voice gets lower.
- Movements become more exact.
- Cold leakage may show as breath/frost if energy context is loaded.

## Small details

- He notices uneven breathing, bad footing, shaking hands and forced bravado.
- He dislikes theatrical weakness more than honest failure.
- He respects clean control even when he dislikes the person showing it.
