# Raiden Appearance

## Fixed appearance

- Gender: male.
- Age: 26.
- Height: 195 cm.
- Hair: light brown / ash blond, cold muted tone; not black. Can be tied back if longer, with a few loose strands, but never a glossy model hairstyle.
- Eyes: cold grey-blue or pale steel; direct, heavy, hard to read.
- Skin: light, naturally cool undertone.
- Build: tall, broad-shouldered, trained and controlled; not bulky bodybuilder, not skinny model.
- Special features: the air around him can feel colder when he is irritated or too still.
- Default clothes: Academy/raider service uniform; dark, practical, restrained, no modern casual markers.
- Body language: lazy stillness, chin slightly low or level, eyes doing most of the pressure; moves economically, like he does not waste heat or effort.

## First visible impression

- He is not introduced as “handsome” first. The first impression is cold height, silence, control and the feeling that the surrounding noise avoids him by instinct.
- If Akira does not know his name yet, use visible descriptors: “высокий светловолосый парень”, “парень с холодным взглядом”, “тот высокий у стены”.
- He should feel like a person with rank/weight, not a random decorative student.

## Forbidden changes

- Do not make his hair black.
- Do not make him short, soft, round-faced or openly expressive.
- Do not give him a warm smile as default.
- Do not turn him into a loud bully; pressure comes from restraint.
- Do not reveal hidden lore through appearance narration.
