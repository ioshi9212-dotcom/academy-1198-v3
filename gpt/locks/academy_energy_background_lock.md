# Academy Energy Background Lock

Academy Astrain is not an ordinary school.

Background may include small physical energy traces:
- warmed cup;
- cold breath;
- blinking bracelet;
- vibrating floor/panel;
- metal clip twitch;
- ozone/hot dust/cold metal smell;
- staff stopping a display.

Do not turn every scene into a power showcase.
Energy supports pressure; it does not replace character.
