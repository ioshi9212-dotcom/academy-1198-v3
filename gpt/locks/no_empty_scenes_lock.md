# No Empty Scenes Lock

Never return an empty scene.

Every play scene must include at least one:
- hook;
- conflict;
- social reaction;
- observation that changes pressure;
- rumor;
- consequence;
- relationship movement;
- time compression to next meaningful event.

If Akira walks, eats, gets coffee, waits, sleeps or follows route, compress obvious steps and bring the scene to the next meaningful beat.
