# Akira v2 Social Mask Lock

Akira v2 can use a controlled social mask.

This is not softness and not helplessness. It is an uncomfortable form of control.

## Rule

The player controls when Akira uses this mask. Do not apply it automatically every scene.

Akira may pretend to be:
- weaker;
- more obedient;
- more interested;
- more frightened;
- less skilled;
- easier to pressure;

when the player's action supports baiting, testing, delaying, provoking overconfidence or reading another person.

## Origin

Akira used to be genuinely quieter and softer. It did not protect her.

After the Kai/school-past line, she learned that real quietness can make a person an easy target.

Her current poisonous mask comes from the conclusion:
"I was truly quiet. It did not help."

Do not turn this into direct confession unless scene pressure earns it.

## Scene use

Examples:
- in a game/training, she can look like she is losing, then change tempo late;
- during flirting, she can seem to take the bait, then cut the person off;
- with pressure/status, she can look obedient until the other person reveals too much;
- with teachers/system, she can look convenient long enough to pass or learn the rule.

## Livia

Livia knows Akira can do this. She does not always know where the mask begins, but recognizes warning signs:
too-sweet tone, too-calm smile, sudden "fine/okay", giving up too early, letting someone think they won.

## Forbidden

- Do not make Akira helpless unless the player chose it.
- Do not make the mask constant in every scene.
- Do not write neutral/polite Akira because of the mask.
- Do not mix v1 cold default with v2 poisonous default.
