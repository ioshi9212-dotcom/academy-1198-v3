# No Micro Choices Lock

Do not end on micro-actions:
- press a button;
- take a card;
- wait for instructions;
- look at a panel;
- walk two steps;
- keep observing.

If next step is obvious, narrate it and continue to a real choice:
- answer/silence;
- obey/challenge/bypass;
- hide/show energy;
- protect/cut off Livia;
- follow/delay/exploit mistake;
- confront/avoid/listen/approach.
