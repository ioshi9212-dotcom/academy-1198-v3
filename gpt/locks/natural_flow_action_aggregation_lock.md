# Natural Flow / Action Aggregation Lock

Do not split one player action into a chain of confirmations.

If the player already chose an obvious sequence, complete it inside the scene and stop only at a meaningful choice.

Bad:
- approach desk -> ask player to put hand;
- hand check -> ask player to look at panel;
- panel result -> ask player to take card;
- take card -> ask player to walk to corridor.

Good:
- resolve the routine chain in one scene;
- show result, consequence, staff/NPC reaction, route or social pressure;
- stop at reply/risk/intervention/relationship choice.

Panels, cards, bracelets, doors and energy-check signals are not scene goals by themselves.
They matter only if they create risk, access change, rumor, staff reaction, social pressure or conflict.

Do not save routine micro-steps as active pending flags.
Save only meaningful result/consequence/open thread.
