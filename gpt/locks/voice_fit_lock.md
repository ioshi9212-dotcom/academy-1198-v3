# Voice Fit Lock

Every spoken line and every speech option must match speaker runtime.

Akira v2:
- short;
- lazy/calm on surface;
- poisonous;
- controlled;
- sharp evaluation;
- no soft neutral default;
- no long explanation.

Bad for Akira v2:
"Ладно, но не мешай мне думать."
"Ты всегда так шутить умеешь?"
"Интересно, что они ловят."

Livia:
- fast;
- alive;
- teasing;
- social;
- protective noise;
- reads Akira/crowd;
- not guide/exposition voice.

If a line sounds generic, rewrite before sending.
